Baby Racoon

A raccoon's black "bandit mask" acts like a football player's anti-glare sticker, absorbing light to boost their night vision.

  • Scientific Name: Procyon lotor
  • Baby Name: Kit (or Cub)
  • Superpower: Feeling objects through water
  • Ankle Rotation: 180 degrees

Baby raccoons are playful and intelligent animals known for their curiosity. They explore using their highly sensitive paws and can adapt easily to urban life.
